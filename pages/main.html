Welcome to Hamro Group: Your Trusted Nepali-Owned Business in Canada


Hamro Group is a proudly registered Canadian company founded by members of the Nepali community. With a deep understanding of the needs and preferences of Nepali customers, we have tailored our services to provide the highest level of quality, convenience, and professionalism. Our mission is to offer a wide range of services that enrich the lives of individuals and businesses alike, with a focus on the Nepali diaspora in Canada. We believe in community, tradition, and trust, and our goal is to bring these values to every service we provide.
